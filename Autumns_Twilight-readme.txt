1. Introduction
----------------

Autumns Twilight Mod features several new NPC joining to the party, new enemies and a plethora of items.

----------------
2. Installation
----------------

Windows:
Copy the content of the ZIP file into your BG2 directory and:

    Execute setup-Autumns_Twilight.exe and follow the instructions

macOS:
Copy the content of the ZIP file into you BG2 directory and:

    Open Terminal then go to game directory
    Grant execution permission to the installation scripts: chmod +x setup-Autumns_Twilight
    Execute the script and follow the instructions: ./setup-Autumns_Twilight.command

-----------------------
3. Features
-----------------------

* NEW JOINABLE NPCS: Dusk (Wizard Slayer), Fall (Beastmaster), Alendir, and Cho'Nuja.

* NEW CONTENT: Over 10 new quests for Shadow of Amn, more than 100 new items, 3 new vendors available, 7 new spells for mages, possibility to upgrade existing items, 6 new race-exclusive kits.

* NEW DUNGEON: Dreach-naga and Rachnidra are abyssal layers locked in an age-old war for access to the Material Plane. Inspired by the settings of Icewind Dale, Planescape: Torment, and Pillars of Eternity, this new dungeon features both old and new animations not originally available in BG2.

* CROSSMOD COMPATIBILITY: Special interactions and unique items for Pai'Na (Artisan), Sirene (Artisan), and Hubelpot (BobTokyo & SConrad).

* AVAILABLE LANGUAGES: English, Spanish, Italian, Polish, German, French, Russian and Chinese (Simplified).

4. Frequently Asked Questions
------------------------------

1. What is Autumn's Twilight?
Autumn's Twilight is a mod for Baldur's Gate 2 EE and BGTEE, designed exclusively for Enhanced Editions, that adds new characters, storylines, and challenges to the original game.

2. How do I install the mod?
   1- Download the mod from https://github.com/evancauteren/bg2-autumns-twilight-mod
   2- Extract the files into your game’s main folder.
   3- Run the installer (setup-Autumns_Twilight.exe) and follow the instructions.

3. Is the mod compatible with other mods?
Yes. For the crossmod content (Pai'Na, Sirene, Hubelpot) to work, those mods must be installed BEFORE Autumn's Twilight.

4. What languages does the mod support?
The mod is available in English and Spanish. Other translations come with a disclaimer that they may not be fully accurate.

5. Where are Dusk and Fall?
Dusk and Fall are in the Waukeen's Promenade of Athkatla. Dusk will approach you after your escape from Irenicus's dungeon, and Fall can be found near the circus's animal cages.

6. Dusk or Fall's sounds/names are mixed up!
Select Dusk or Fall and press the K key.

7. Does the mod include romances?
No, the mod offers deep friendship paths with the 4 joinable NPCs.

8. I already have a save started. Can I continue it?
Yes, but some content (like the new dungeon available after the Underdark) might be inaccessible if you are already too far along.

6. Changelog
-------------------

Version 1.4.5 (Current)

- New Content:
  * New animations: Venomous Scolopendra and Alaghi.
  * Interactions added for Fall's summons (Daren, Sinna, Tabitha, Kitochi, Voragor, and Kakuto).
  * Crossmod content integrated with: Pai'Na, Sirene, and Hubelpot. Includes unique items like Extract of Scolopendra, Cloak of Ilmater's Perseverance, and Magic Berries.
  * Note: New banters remain in English to prevent automated translation errors.

- Fixes & Balancing:
  * Nerfed Rachnidra enemies on NORMAL difficulty (Mesothel, Mega Centipede, Ettercap Hunter, various Spiders and Weavers).
  * Fixed Fall's interactions with the Bhaalspawn in ToB.
  * Fixed Sir Ryan Trawl's script regarding friendly fire hostility.
  * Alendir and Cho'Nuja can no longer be summoned if they died/left in SoA.
  * Refactored code for improved readability and maintainability.

Version 1.3.0
- Bug fixes (Verminpiercer, version number in log, Dusk hostility fix, anti-duplication patches).
- Added remaining banters for Alendir and Cho'Nuja.
